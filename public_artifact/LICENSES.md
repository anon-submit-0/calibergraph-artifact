author-created code.

documentation are released under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0

https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

MultiGov release records. It does not grant access to, or rights over, any
withheld private rows, private mappings, credentials, or source-system data.

author-created source code in this artifact is released under the Apache
License 2.0:

https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0

retain their original licenses and attribution requirements. Their own license
files and provenance records take precedence over the defaults above.
